Getting Ready
For a Full-time or Freelance Career

Knowing how to design and develop web & mobile apps is required, but it's not enough to get you a good job that aligns with your goals and talents. This program helps prepare you for the job search.

If you are interested in a freelance career, this program helps you put a professional infrastructure and approach to working on your own for a variety of clients.

About

This program is available to Portland Code School students enrolled in a career-oriented class like Full-Stack JavaScript, iOS Development, or Front End Development.

Networking

You could submit your resume through a form and get in line with hundreds of applicants. Or, you could connect with people who are looking for people to hire through a network of helpful, professional collegues.

Interviewing

You interview many times for a particular job. Some are informal, some are almost ceremonial. Each engagement has a purpose and a rhythm

  • Casual conversations and the language of value
  • Behavioral interviewing
  • Good answers to vague questions
  • Following up

All current Portland Code School full-stack, mobile, or front-end students are eligible for the Job Readiness Program. In order to be accepted into the program, students need to fulfill the requirements of their technical coursework and have their instructorâ€™s approval.

Students accepted into the Portland Code School Job Readiness Program will receive additional training in the following areas:

  • Skill inventory and life goals - knowing what your goals are and what you can offer
  • Researching potential employeers - matching your goals with great places to work
  • Professional networking - creating a social network presence, participating in community events, and understanding how building meaningful, ethical and reciprocal relationships can help people remember your value when they create a job opening
  • Crafting a unique resume and why this basic tool is more important than ever
  • Creating, maintaining, and leveraging your LinkedIn profile to tell your story
  • Creating a compelling portfolio website that tells not only what youâ€™ve done already, but how quickly you learn and what value you can provide to potential employers and clients
  • Interviewing skills - lots of practice in fielding questions about your hard and soft skills, demonstrating your technical prowess on the fly in front of a whiteboard, and following up to stand out from the crowd and continue building your professional network

Students who successfully complete both their technical coursework and the job readiness program will be recommended to placement and staffing partners and introduced to companies in our employer network.
